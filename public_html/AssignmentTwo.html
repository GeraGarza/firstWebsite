Common Reasons for Taking Course
It seems like most people in this class are interested in taking this class to learn about how media affects us in our day-to-day lives. This class is different than other computer science courses, and that sparks curiosity in the students. We are also interested in looking at computer science in a different light, as in seeing the affects of technology instead of trying to create something new. From my observation, it seems most people are interested in this class to learn about the influences media, wether it is the influence of the medium or the content itself. It was also clear, given we heavily rely on media sources for information, that people are interested in how we construct different paradigms given what is fed to us through what we follow. We are also focused on how technology plays a role in our lives, espcially given we are computer science majors.
Common Media Usage
Just about everyone in the class listens to music, TV, Netflix, and plays games. The specifics vary from student to student, but in general usage remains the same. There is also similarities in the contant we like to see. It is clear that that what the mainstream media pushes tends to hold popular for most people. It really makes you question who governs our interests, wether we make the mainstream media or not.
